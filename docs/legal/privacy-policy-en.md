# Privacy Policy — SERVIX

**Last Updated:** April 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **SERVIX**, a salon management platform. We are committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the Saudi Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL), Royal Decree M/19.

## 2. Data We Collect

| Type | Examples | Legal Basis |
|------|---------|------------|
| **Account Data** | Name, email, phone number | Contract performance |
| **Salon Data** | Business name, CR number, VAT ID | Legal obligation (ZATCA) |
| **Booking Data** | Appointments, services, prices | Contract performance |
| **Payment Data** | Transaction references (no card data stored) | Contract performance |
| **Technical Data** | IP address, browser type, activity logs | Legitimate interest |

## 3. How We Use Your Data

- Providing salon management services
- Processing bookings and payments
- Issuing electronic invoices (ZATCA compliance)
- Sending appointment notifications
- Platform improvement and user experience
- Legal and regulatory compliance

## 4. Data Sharing

We share data only with:
- **ZATCA** — Electronic invoicing (legal requirement)
- **Moyasar** — Payment processing
- **Google Firebase** — Mobile push notifications
- **Hetzner Cloud** — Secure infrastructure hosting

❌ **We never sell your data to third parties.**

## 5. Your Rights (PDPL)

- **Right of Access** — Request a copy of your data
- **Right of Correction** — Update inaccurate data
- **Right of Erasure** — Request account deletion (30-day cooling period)
- **Right to Object** — Object to data processing

> **Note:** Tax-related data is retained for 7 years per ZATCA regulations.

## 6. Data Security

- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256-GCM)
- Secret management via HashiCorp Vault
- Strict CSP and CSRF protection
- Continuous security monitoring

## 7. Data Retention

| Type | Retention Period |
|------|-----------------|
| Account data | Until deletion + 30 days |
| Invoices | 7 years (legal requirement) |
| Audit logs | 3 years |
| Backups | 90 days |
